Founding a company

“StarkGotSole” was an event business I founded in the later years of high school. I recognized a need locally to have an event where people could buy/sell/trade their streetwear. It was an immediate success and grew quickly.

Within the first several shows we outgrew the original church gym location, and held events at the University of Akron ballroom. I hired staff to help with each event (ticketing, concessions, police, customer service), ran all social media, and built the website to further our reach.

From the start it was about community, and throughout our five years of operation, after each show we donated 10% of profits to purchasing brand new shoes for our local homeless shelter. I donated hundreds of shoes, connected many people, and learned valuable lessons along the way.
